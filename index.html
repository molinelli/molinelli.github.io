Welcome to Molinelli Vintage!

We are so excited to have you here because it is our biggest passion to give pre-owned pieces a second life. Our selection includes vintage clothes and second hand of prestigious and authentic brands.

If you want to ask a question, leave feedback or suggest an idea, don't hesitate to contact us. Happy shopping :)
